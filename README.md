# or-demo

This is a demo for modeling and solving problems in operation research.

## Dependency

1. [ortools](https://github.com/google/or-tools)
1. [osqp](https://github.com/oxfordcontrol/osqp)

### Others
numpy, scipy

## Model

### Planing
1. economic planing
1. farm planing
1. mining
1. power generation

### Pricing
1. agricultural pricing

### TSP & VRP
1. milk collection
1. lost baggage

### Location
1. depot location
1. decentralisation

### Partitioning
1. market sharing

### Constraint
1. optimize constraint
1. efficiency analysis
